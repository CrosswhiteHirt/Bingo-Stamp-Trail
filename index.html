About the Game

Lucky Wave Bingo turns familiar bingo cards into a compact resource-planning puzzle. A run starts with limited gold, a small number of calls, and active card slots. Bingo is not a finish line; it is an income event that helps you buy cards, manage call packs, and survive rising upkeep.

The design uses fictional gold and ledger pressure to keep the focus on calm planning: when to spend, when to wait, and how much risk the next ledger can support.

Key Features

  • Continuous survival loop based on gold, calls, market cards, and ledger upkeep.
  • Token calls including column, wild, exact number, bonus, and market choices.
  • Economic upgrade paths such as dividends, interest, long-game scaling, and extra table slots.
  • Readable landscape SpriteKit interface with a clean stamp-and-card style.
